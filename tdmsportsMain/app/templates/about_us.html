About us

Company

Established in the year 2001 by Tyler de Mier, TDM Sports has long been established as the leading Basque specialist retailer of fashionable branded, sports and casual wear. What started as a single shop in Bilbao, Biscay has now expanded across the Basque Country, and by 2006, TDM Sports has opened a total of 56 stores. By 2011, TDM Sports has dominated most of the EU, primarily in Germany, Ireland, France, Netherlands and Spain, with Denmark and Sweden to follow suit. At this moment in time, TDM Sports was already regarded as the most innovative visual merchandiser of sportswear with the best and most exclusive stylish range.

Fast forward to 2016, TDM Sports officially launched its first store in Asia, right here in Malaysia. In November, TDM Sports opened its first flagship store in Pavilion Elite, Kuala Lumpur, and its third store in Asia in Sunway Velocity, Cheras.

TDM Sports looks forward to a progressive development in Asia in the days to come.
